Frequently Asked Questions

How often should I use a red light therapy belt?

Most experts recommend 10-20 minutes per session, once or twice daily. Consistency matters more than duration‚Äîdaily use yields better results than occasional long sessions.

Is red light therapy safe?

Yes. Red and near-infrared light are non-ionizing, meaning they don't damage DNA like UV rays. It's considered very safe when used as directed.

How long until I see results?

Some people feel immediate relief, but most notice improvements within 1-2 weeks of consistent use. Chronic conditions may take longer.

Can I use it with other therapies?

Absolutely. Red light therapy pairs well with stretching, massage, ice/heat therapy, and compression. Just space them out throughout the day.

Do I need eye protection?

While direct eye exposure isn't dangerous with these low-powered devices, it's a good practice to close your eyes or look away during treatment.
