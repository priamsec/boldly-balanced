What Is Pilates?

Pilates is a low-impact exercise method developed by Joseph Pilates in the early 1900s. It focuses on:

  • Core strength
  • Full-body conditioning
  • Flexibility and mobility
  • Mind-body connection
  • Breath control

Why It's exploded in 2026

1. It's Low-Impact

Unlike high-intensity workouts, Pilates is gentle on joints while delivering serious results. Perfect for all fitness levels and ages.

2. It Complements Other Workouts

Runners do Pilates for better form. Weightlifters do it for mobility. It's the perfect addition to any fitness routine.

3. At-Home Options Are Amazing Now

You don't need a studio. Quality reformers and mat workouts are available at home.

4. Mental Health Benefits

Pilates reduces stress, improves focus, and creates a meditative state through breathwork.

Mat vs. Reformer: Which Is Right for You?

Mat Pilates

  • Affordable ($20-50 for essentials)
  • Great for beginners
  • Can do anywhere
  • Start with: A quality yoga mat and resistance bands

Reformer Pilates

  • Uses a machine with springs
  • More variety in exercises
  • Typically $1,000-2,000 for home reformers
  • Great for: Those ready to invest in their practice
